If You Believe, You Will Receive Whatever You Ask For In Prayer. Matthew 21:22.
Few years ago I joined campus one on the top institutions in my country.Deep down I had a real joy,it was a dream come true.It felt as though I was at the top of The world.During my first at the university I was so focused with my education That that year I was among the few students who appeared in the pass list in My class,I learned this when the results were released by the university’s examination board. When the second year came I was so over confident.Afraid to miss the college fun I was Eager to try new things.Among them was doing drugs.Marijuana also made it to my list Among few others.After all,I had seen and heard many students who were in drugs And were just doing fine with their studies,that kind of thinking gave me the green light To pursue that kind of life.During second year first semester is that in my mind Everything my education was going right but in reality things were going south.I was Stuck by my first years good performance,thinking that I was smart enough to excel yet again.Literally drugs deluded me.During the end of that semester’s year I was Seeing darkness in examination room and that is when epiphanies of how I made terrible decisions.After few weeks holiday I returned to school again.What was Amusing me at that second semester is that I never changed my life style despite The terrible experience I did face earlier.I was caught up in a drug trap that I had No formula how to escape.Sad life began to hit since I knew I was destined to failure If that toxic behaviour persists.Yet again I failed in exams the way I never Imagined In my life I would.I was standing at the point of facing discontinuation at this point In college.I was so depressed and hated my life.It was a real pain,I never thought I was that foolish.I couldn’t see how my education will progress anymore.I hit the Rock bottom,my thoughts run dry not knowing how my situation could change for Better.Ultimately my last decision was to run to God.At this point I was seeking Comfort,I did tell myself I got it wrong and God won’t bother changing my Situation.I did knew choices has consequences so I had to live with my painful Failures.At that point I quit attending lectures.I wanted to focus on Jesus.After I tried using my own knowledge had what I got was failure.I did start attending Church regularly.Yes,my circles also began to enlarge since I was meeting College students who were living for God.My wounds started healing slowly since I was relating With other kinds of friends who had a different mindset.As the time moved on,I made a decision To start going to the university library when am not involved in church work.What made me go to Library was fascinating.Since I had enrolled to pursue business and information technology Course,I had heard a lecturer who was introducing us to C programming language in first say That whoever would get the things he was teaching us right that knowledge would enable The person to earn a decent living in the internet.I did a research and I couldn’t Described my feelings using words to learn one didn’t need a college degree to excel In computing world.Since my parents had gifted me with a laptop in my early days at Campus,I downloaded free tutorials from the internet .The main objective was to be A software engineer.I focused on this knowing that I had no any other choice.Initially Things were very hard.I gave up many times along the way but some small voice In told me to continue trying.After all I had no options so I had to do it whether I liked it or not.Eventually,after long trials I began understanding technical concepts.This Motivated me a lot.How cannot it be when things started to got easier.There was so much Hope,I had placed my name to the class of failure where I thought come back would never Be possible.It was hard to believe.To cut the long story short is that as we speak I am Among the online merchants which are doing well.Not only did I end up being a exceptional Web developer I also operate as a data analyst helping struggling companies sit at the top Of the game.Among the best decisions I have ever made in my life was to have a personal Relationship with Jesus Christ and also to make a decision to drop out of college.Today I am 100 % convinced their nothing God cannot do.I did see him move mountains In my life and their nothing I can offer to him except my own heart,He has it all.Dear Reader if you’re at a position where nothing seems to work in your life,God is waiting For you with open arms ready to give you a rest you have never found in this world.God Has it all.Do not allow doubts in mind discourage you from seeking God’s intervention,in fact Doubts are enemy’s tool which he uses to imprison God’s people from their true purpose In this life.The good thing about God is that he will speak the language that your minds understands To inform you about you genuine purpose in this world.The joy you will find waking up to advance Your real purpose is out of this world.If it is the money or peace is what your seeking will find away To find you wherever you might be.In Case you’re wondering where to begin a relationship with God worry no more.Just use google consistently for two months.At first it will be hard to do this But in due time you will experience the real power of the creator of the universe speaking to you.When it is all said and done that will be the best decision you’ll ever make in your life.
